ABOUT US

MMMUT (formerly Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, Gorakhpur) was established in 1962 with the primary motive to impart quality education and train the youth to take up the future challenges of evolving India. The first and only residential technical University in U.P. with a total strength of 7000 students is presently running seven B.Tech. programs along with M.B.A., M.C.A., M.Tech. in 12 disciplines and fulltime Ph.D. programme in number of disciplines as part of Quality Improvement Programme of MHRD and TEQIP-II project. MMMUT has become a leading institution in engineering and management with departments as centers of excellence, imparting high quality education and providing ambience for growth of professionals as excellent human beings as well.

Entrepreneurship Cell is students society of MMMUT which focuses primarily to promote entrepreneurial abilities among students. To accomplish this periodic guest lectures, expert talks, workshops and various events of the domain are frequently organized by the cell. Entrepreneurship Cell creates a network of students, alumni, teachers and entrepreneurs together to help out any startup being raised by a student anyway.

Mission

LAs Ecell, MMMUT ,we believe in adding values to individuals by bringing young thoughts into action. Entrepreneurs fired with ideas embody the true passion for growing, we try to achieve that through nurturing the ideas of advancing innovation with compassion and enhancing productivity.We are incorporating some interventions in this direction to strengthen our multifarious programmers.

Vision

We aim at nurturing the passion for self employment that will enable a critical drive for innovation that incorporates edifying the entrepreneurial ecosystem and nourishing team building skills. Besides, Analyzing pilot feasibility and providing a detailed methodology that will foster the startup and self-employment culture among aspiring technopreneurs. We aim to continue our effort in the direction of creating enterprising youth who are capable of surviving in an entrepreneurial society.

Values

We at E-Cell MMMUT, believe in high professional ethics and human values, and emphasize on their practice in our operations. Our core values are-Pursuit of excellence, learning by doing, Transparency, Integrity, Mutual Respect.

Flash of Genius(FoG)

This event is for showcasing your creative as well as analytical skills. In this event, first you have to pass the aptitude test leading you to the next round of group discussion round and then to the last round of personal Interview for your idea presentation



Fresher’s Talk

This event provides a platform to the freshers to express their views And give wings to their notions. In this you have to send video clips answering the various questions about college life and the most amusing one will be featured is the fresher’s talk video of this year.



Encuesta De Criquet

In this event participants take polls related to IPL matches that are held on that day. This event have 2 rounds, first is an online quiz for your knowledge in the field of cricket and then the final round in which after completion of the match the participants are awarded points based on their poll. And the one who gets the highest points after the completion of IPL, is the winner.

Campus ambassador

This event is for focusing the participant’s knowledge about the college campus, their creativity and communication skills. This event consists of 2 rounds in which the first round is a quiz about the college campus and the second one is a extempore round that leads the participants to the winning of the event.

Market Kshetra

This event is for showcasing your managerial and technical skills. In this you have to generate your own ads and advertise for it on spot. This event comprises 3 rounds first is pen-paper round and then to the next round for your product advertisement(on spot), that leads you to the final round of video advertisement.

Logo Maestro


This event consists of some startling and phenomenal questions Based on logos of various assorted brands and companies. This event consist 5 Rounds in which 3 rounds are Pen-paper Rounds and 2 are Buzzer Rounds. The Participating team will be of two members.
